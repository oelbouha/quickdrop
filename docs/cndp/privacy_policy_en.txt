## Introduction
QuickDrop is a modern Flutter application that connects travelers with people who need to ship packages. We collect and process personal data to provide peer-to-peer package delivery, secure payments, notifications, messaging, and support. We comply with Moroccan Law 09-08 and CNDP requirements.

## Data Collected
- Name, email, phone number
- Delivery address, profile address
- Geolocation (GPS coordinates for shipments/trips)
- Photos (profile, delivery proof; stored on Supabase)
- Device ID, Firebase UID
- Payment references (Stripe tokens, subscription status)
- Vehicle info (plate number, model, driver number for couriers)

## Purposes
- Account creation and management (senders and couriers)
- Shipment and trip listings
- Real-time shipment tracking
- Secure payment processing via Stripe
- In-app notifications and messaging
- Profile management and statistics
- Customer support

## Storage & Processors
- Firebase (Google LLC): Authentication, Firestore, Cloud Storage, Cloud Functions, Messaging
- Supabase: image/photo storage
- Stripe: payment processing

## Data Transfers
Data may be transferred outside Morocco to Google Firebase (USA/Europe), Supabase (Europe), and Stripe (USA/Europe) under contractual safeguards and security measures.

## Security Measures
- TLS/HTTPS encryption in transit
- Firebase encryption at rest
- Firebase Authentication
- Role-based access control and permissions
- Secure image storage on Supabase
- Secure payments via Stripe
- Regular backups and access controls

## Retention
- Account: until deletion by user
- Shipments/trips: 5 years
- Photos: 1 year
- Logs/statistics: 1 year
